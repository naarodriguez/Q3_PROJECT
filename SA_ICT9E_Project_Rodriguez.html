One of the most common accidents that occur in places are Fire accidents. It usually happens when people may have triggered something flammable or left something near fire. To avoid any of these situations, here are some ways you can avoid having having fire in your home.

Use rechargable light instead of candles when there is a brown out

Candles are one of the biggest sources of fire accidents, anything flammable near it can cause huge flames.

Avoid having big amount of fuel at home

When fuel is near fire, it can cause huge flames as well

Have a fire extinguisher near you

When fire appears in your area, it is recommended to have a fire extinguisher near you for fast access

Never leave the kitchen unattended

There may be open stoves in the kitchen that can start a fire, always check the area if everything is turned off

Unplug all cords when not using

Cords may heat up when left on the electrical outlet and it is best to remove it before it overheats
